About Me

Herwin
Cabo

I'm Herwin Cabo — a filmmaker and visual storyteller based in Watford, England. I don't just point a camera at the world. I chase the moments that disappear in seconds and make them last forever.

Every project starts with a feeling — the energy of a crowd, the silence before a first dance, the light hitting just right. My job is to be ready when that moment arrives, and to capture it in a way that still gives you goosebumps years later.

From weddings to 18th birthdays, corporate events to personal stories — whatever the occasion, the goal is always the same: create something that moves people.
