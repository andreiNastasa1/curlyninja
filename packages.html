What is a Same Day Edit?

A Same Day Edit is one of the most magical additions to any wedding. Your footage is filmed and edited on the day itself — then screened for your guests during the evening reception. Everyone in the room relives the morning together. People laugh, cry, and cheer. It turns your reception into something truly unforgettable.
